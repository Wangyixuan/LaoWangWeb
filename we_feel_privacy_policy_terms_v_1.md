_Last updated: [DATE]_

WeFeel respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how WeFeel (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. What WeFeel Is

WeFeel is an anonymous emotional sharing application. It allows users to express how they feel and to support others’ emotions.

WeFeel is **not** a medical, psychological, or therapeutic service.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- Short emotional text you choose to share

We do **not** require real names, profile photos, or personal descriptions.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- Basic device information (such as device type, operating system version)
- App usage events (for stability and performance purposes only)

We do **not** collect:
- Precise location data
- Contacts
- Photos or media files without your action
- Health or medical records

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

- Display emotional content within the app
- Allow others to show support for shared emotions
- Maintain app functionality, safety, and performance
- Prevent abuse, spam, or harmful behavior

We do **not** use your data for advertising or tracking across apps or websites.

---

## 4. Anonymity and Emotional Content

Emotional content shared on WeFeel is anonymous by default.

Please do not include personal identifying information in your emotional text. Once shared, emotional content may be visible to others in an aggregated or anonymous form.

---

## 5. Sensitive Situations

WeFeel does not provide crisis support or professional mental health services.

If you are experiencing severe distress or are in danger, please seek help from local emergency services or qualified professionals.

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no system is completely secure. By using WeFeel, you acknowledge and accept this risk.

## 7. Data Retention

- You may request deletion of your content or account at any time

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

WeFeel is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
